Usage:

  npm-used CATEGORY OPTIONS

    Shows you a list of the most dependended on modules from the people
    in the category choosen.

    CATEGORY defaults to `DEFAULT` which by default is an empty category

    OPTIONS
      --help : your looking at it

---------

  npm-used add AUTHOR CATEGORY

    Adds the author (this should be an npm owner name) to the CATEGORY

    CATEGORY will default to `DEFAULT`

---------

  npm-used rm AUTHOR CATEGORY

    Removes the author from the CATEGORY

    CATEGORY will default to `DEFAULT`

---------

  npm-used ls CATEGORY

    Shows the authors in the category.

    CATEGORY will default to `DEFAULT`
